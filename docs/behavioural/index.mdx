Here are 15 key behavioural interview questions that you might be asked during your Nordstrom interview. These questions are designed to understand how you have handled situations in the past and how you might handle them in the future.

**Master the [STAR Method](https://www.themuse.com/advice/star-interview-method) (Situation, Task, Action, Result) or [CAR Method](https://www.indeed.com/career-advice/interviewing/car-method-interview) (Context, Action, Result) for these questions to give compelling answers.**
