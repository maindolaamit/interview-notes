Here are some of the behavioural interview questions that you might be asked during your interview. These questions are designed to understand how you have handled situations in the past and how you might handle them in the future.

Figuring out the [STAR](https://www.themuse.com/advice/star-interview-method) method for these questions will help you answer them better.

1. [What is your greatest Strength](./greatest-strength.mdx)
2. [What is your greatest weakness](./greatest-weakness.mdx)
3. [Tell me a time where you failed or made a mistake?](./failed.mdx)
4. [Why Should We Hire You?](./why-hire-you.mdx)
4. [Why do you want to work here?](./why-work-here.mdx)
4. [Describe your most challenging project](./challenging-project.mdx)
4. [Tell me about something you've accomplished that you are proud of](./accomplished)
4. [What do you like to do outside your work](./outside-work.mdx)
4. [Describe your leadership style](./leadership-style.mdx)
4. [Tell me about a time where you worked with a difficult person](./difficult-person.mdx)
4. [Tell me about a time where you disagreed with someone](./difficult-person.mdx)
4. [Tell me about a time where you had to manage conflicts](./difficult-person.mdx)
4. [Tell me about a time where you surpassed people's expectations](./difficult-person.mdx)
4. [Tell me about a time where you had to handle pressure](./difficult-person.mdx)
4. [Tell me about a time where you had to learn something quickly](./difficult-person.mdx)
